# UK State Pension Calendar App

A modern, privacy-focused web app to calculate and export your UK State Pension payment calendar. Built with SvelteKit, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS.

## Features
- Calculates your UK State Pension age and payment schedule
- Handles all official payment cycles (28-day, 14-day, 7-day, 91-day)
- Adjusts for UK bank holidays (latest data from GOV.UK)
- Exports to CSV and ICS (calendar) formats
- Works offline and can be installed as a PWA
- All calculations run in your browser; no data leaves your device

## Quick Start
1. Open the app in your browser (desktop or mobile)
2. Enter your NI code suffix and date of birth
3. Choose your payment cycle and date range
4. View, print, or export your payment calendar
